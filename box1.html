Property Description

Welcome to this breathtaking waterfront villa, where luxury meets coastal living. This architectural masterpiece offers an unparalleled lifestyle with panoramic ocean views from nearly every room. Completed in 2022, this contemporary residence features soaring ceilings, walls of glass, and seamless indoor-outdoor living spaces.

The main level showcases an open-concept design with a gourmet kitchen featuring top-of-the-line appliances, custom cabinetry, and a large center island. The adjacent great room opens to an expansive terrace with an infinity pool that appears to merge with the ocean beyond. The primary suite is a true sanctuary with a private balcony, spa-like bathroom, and custom walk-in closets.

Additional features include a home theater, wine cellar, fitness room, and smart home technology throughout. The property is situated on 1.2 acres with 120 feet of ocean frontage, offering privacy and direct beach access. Meticulously landscaped grounds include multiple outdoor entertaining areas, a summer kitchen, and a fire pit lounge.

This exceptional residence represents the pinnacle of coastal luxury living, combining architectural excellence with an irreplaceable location. Don't miss this rare opportunity to own one of the most prestigious properties on the California coastline.
